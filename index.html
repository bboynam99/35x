HOW TO DELEGATE?

After the launch of Shelley Incentivized Testnet on 9th December 2019, you can delegate your testnet ADA to X Pool Cardano through Daedalus and Yoroi testnet wallet.

To know more about Shelley Incentivized Testnet and to download testnet wallet visit staking.cardano.org and tesnet.iohkdev.io
